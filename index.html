CrashTO: A Visualization of Vehicle Collisions in the City of Toronto

Number of Collisions per 500 meters

CrashTO is a tool that compiles automobile collision data across the City of Toronto.

By creating a hexagonal tesselation, each representing 500 meters of space, hotspots emerge representing areas with high proportions of collisions.

How to use:

Use the checkboxes above to turn on and off hexagonal and/or collision point layers to improve your understanding of collisions across the city. You can also use checkbox associated with the legend to turn on and off the legend. By searching for a location in Toronto via the geocoder, users can analyze their neighbourhoods and the collisions near them. By hovering over and clicking layers, users can better understand the type of collision associated with collision points, and the amount of collisions per ward.
